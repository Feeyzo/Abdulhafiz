// About me

Developer. Designer. Founder.

Building software that matters across web, mobile, and beyond.

I'm Abdulhafiz — a software developer and product designer based in Kano, Nigeria. I specialize in full-stack web and mobile development, with a strong eye for UX and product design.

I'm the founder of Riderr, a logistics platform connecting individuals and businesses with verified delivery riders across Nigeria. I've also worked with LOGI-Q Technologies and NITDA, sharpening my skills across real-world engineering challenges.

I graduated from Bayero University Kano and have since dedicated my career to building products that solve genuine Nigerian — and global — problems.
